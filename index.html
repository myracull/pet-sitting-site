Meet Dot

Hey there! I'm Julia, but I also go by Dot! I have had the privilege of caring for many different pets of my own while growing up, and now I get to extend my love for animals to my community. Pet sitting was something I naturally fell into, starting by taking care of my neighbor's pets in Yucaipa, California. Slowly, I have extended my services to Mentone, Redlands, Beaumont, Highland, and beyond. I love meeting new pets and their families, and knowing I get to provide a service for fellow animal-lovers. I hope you will consider me the next time you need a loving pet sitter, and I look forward to meeting you and your companions!
